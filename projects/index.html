Capstone

For my last full year of academia I had the pleasure and opportunity to lead a group of collegues in the development of Free-Space Optical Communication system. We underwent reaseach and development to fully meet deliverables set out by our supervising professor while achieving a fully functional prototype.The idea behind our communication system was to implent a fibre optic's without the fibre optic line. This was done by using a laser diode with TTL modulation transmitting a signal to a recieving photodiode.

Cadence Design

For my cadence design project I was tasked with building and optimizing a 6-bit full adder from the transistor level. To tackle this I spent alot of time researching papers and development of a traditional full adder and looked for areas where transistors could be reduced. I quickly was able to identify that by using a SERF adder I could lower the traditional gates used from 28 down to 12. With this drastic reduction the speed of the full adder was able to be optimized to 307.5ps from 15ns. I then produced the associated layout design for the full-adder and stayed with in 130nm technology parameters.
