Data Analytics

Exploratory data analysis I have undergone for school and own leasiure

I had the opportunity to explore a very large data set of my choosing and to refine/clean the data. Naturally being a hockey fan I choose to use collected NHL data from the last 10 years and perform an exploratory data analysis (EDA). From this I was able to look at various collected stats and form a couple of interesting conclusions on average shift length, best coach, best team... etc. To present my findings I created a Tableau Dashboard as it is more visually appearing for the average viewer rather than looking at code and a result. From this project I grew an appreciation for advanced stats in sports as I got to see stats from teams at a lower level rather than just if they won or lost.
