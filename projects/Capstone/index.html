Capstone

My graduating capstone project

For my last full year of academia I had the pleasure and opportunity to lead a group of colleagues in the development of Free-Space Optical Communication system. We underwent research and development to fully meet deliverables set out by our supervising professor while achieving a fully functional prototype. The idea behind our communication system was to implement the idea of fibre optic without the fibre optic line. This was done by using a laser diode with TTL modulation transmitting a signal to a receiving photodiode. We worked with a 980nm Laser diode as safety was at the upmost importance and at 980nm we could keep the Max Permissible Exposure low with minimizing the affects to our overall goal.
