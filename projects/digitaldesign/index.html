Digital and Analog design of a 14 transitor adder

For my cadence design project I was tasked with building and optimizing a 6-bit full adder from the transistor level. To tackle this I spent a lot of time researching papers and development of a traditional full adder and looked for areas where transistors could be reduced. I quickly was able to identify that by using a SERF adder I could lower the traditional gates used from 28 down to 14. With this drastic reduction the speed of the full adder was able to be optimized to 307.5ps from 15ns. I then produced the associated layout design for the full-adder and stayed with in 130nm technology parameters.
